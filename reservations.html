RESERVATIONS

We accept bookings online for tables of 1 to 4. Please note that there is limited seating availability online, If your preferred time is unavailable, we ask you to please call the restaurant directly. Our reservations office is open Monday to Friday from 9am and from 2pm on Saturday and Sunday. For immediate reservations please call the restaurant directly.
Please be aware we do not take bookings for the bar at Rockpool Bar & Grill and reservations apply only to restaurant tables.


Group Bookings

For bookings of 8 or more people we will require credit card details to secure the booking. No deposit will be processed on your card, but should you need to cancel your booking we ask that you give us at least 24 hours notice, or incur a cancellation fee of $50 per person. A discretionary 10% service fee is applied to the final bill for groups of 10 guests or more.
